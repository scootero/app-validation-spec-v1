# Features

## Habit stacking

Chain small habits in sequence so each one triggers the next. Build a morning routine that actually sticks.

## Smart reminders

Get nudges at the right time — not so many that you ignore them. Reminders adapt to your completion patterns.

## Streak tracking

See your current streak, longest streak, and weekly completion rate at a glance. Celebrate milestones without guilt.

## Cross-platform sync

Start on iOS, check in on Android, review on web. Your stacks and progress stay in sync everywhere.

## Weekly insights

A simple Sunday summary shows what worked, what slipped, and one suggestion for the week ahead.

## Privacy first

Your habit data stays yours. No selling data to advertisers. Export anytime.
