# FAQ

## Is there a free trial?

Yes. Habit Stack includes a 7-day free trial on the monthly plan. You can cancel anytime before the trial ends and you will not be charged.

## Which platforms are supported?

Habit Stack is available on iOS, Android, and web. Your account syncs across all platforms with a single subscription.

## How is this different from a simple to-do app?

To-do apps track tasks. Habit Stack is built for **recurring behaviors** — the small daily actions that compound into real change. Stacking, streaks, and routine templates are designed specifically for habit formation.

## Can I use it for a morning routine and an evening routine?

Absolutely. Create multiple stacks — one for morning, one for evening, or themed stacks like "workout days" or "wind-down."

## What happens if I miss a day?

Your streak resets, but your history and insights remain. Habit Stack is designed to encourage consistency, not shame. You can enable "flex days" in settings for travel or rest days.

## How do I cancel my subscription?

Cancel anytime in App Store or Google Play subscription settings, or from your account page on the web. Access continues until the end of your billing period.
